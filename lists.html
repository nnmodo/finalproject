Need an item list?

Help yourself with our basic necessities list below!

Some items may not be neccesary.

  • Sleeping bag
  • Reliable backpack
  • Tent
  • Blankets for warmth
  • Durable water bottle
  • Portable stove
  • Meal ready to eat, or MREs
  • Shower bag containing wipes, towels, and other toiletries
  • Pocket multi-tool

Camping is always fun, but it's better when you're prepared. Always make sure you keep to your list, and stay well supplied.
