General tips specialized for you!

Take these tips and use them to your advantage future camper.

Whether you're experienced or inexperienced, these will help you!

  • Look for gear that is a known reliable brand
  • Wear durable, and quick to dry clothing
  • Do some research on the campground you are planning to visit, that way you will know the best time to visit.
  • Pick the best overall campsite
  • Take a first aid class beforehand
  • Have a safety plan
  • Carry a method to get in contact with emergency services, such as an emergency transponder
  • Pack extra food
  • Don't sleep with food in your tent, it will attract wildlife

    • Simply do your research, and always plan ahead!

Though camping is fun, it's critical to plan ahead, and be prepared. You don't want to be stuck in the wilderness while unprepared. That is a recipe for disaster.
